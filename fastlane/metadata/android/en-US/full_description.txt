HydroTracker - Modern Water Intake Tracker

Stay properly hydrated with HydroTracker, a beautifully designed water tracking app that helps you monitor your daily water intake and build healthy hydration habits.

Key Features

Smart Tracking
- Log water intake with pre-defined containers or custom amounts
- Personalized daily hydration goals based on your profile
- Comprehensive analytics with daily and historical statistics
- Track patterns and insights to improve your hydration habits

Intelligent Reminders
- Context-aware notifications that respect your sleep schedule
- Customizable reminder intervals and active hours
- Precise timing for consistent hydration throughout the day

Beautiful Design
- Modern Material 3 design with dynamic theming
- Dark mode support for comfortable viewing
- Guided onboarding to get you started quickly
- Home screen widgets for quick progress updates

Privacy Focused
- All data stored locally on your device
- No cloud sync or data collection
- No usage tracking or analytics
- Works completely offline

Customization Options
- Flexible container presets for instant logging
- Activity-based goal recommendations
- Comprehensive user settings and preferences
- Optional profile photo support
